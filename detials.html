Sita Ramam

Director: Hanu Raghavapudi

Genre: Romance/Drama

About the movie

Sita Ramam is a heartwarming love story that transcends time, set against the backdrop of an army officer's journey in the 1960s.

"Sita Ramam" explores the enduring power of love, duty, and sacrifice. Set during a tumultuous period, the movie follows Lieutenant Ram, a soldier stationed at the border, as he embarks on a journey to find Sita, a mysterious woman who writes him letters. Their love story is intertwined with themes of patriotism, loyalty, and the courage to stand by one's promises. With beautiful cinematography and a soulful soundtrack, the film captures the essence of timeless love that defies the boundaries of war and distance.
